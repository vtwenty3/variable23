New Begining

What a sunset ...- I was thinking, I felt the warm wind coming from "Hatzenback". It was late summer, unusually quiet for a racetrack. Suddenly the silence was pierced by the scream of 800 horsepowers coming from the engine of a GT-R R34. Goosebumps, it was on the edge of control, no traction control, no useless electronics, just raw power...
